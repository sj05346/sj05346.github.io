Endsomnia

  • Year: 2020

  • Role: UX Designer, UI Designer

Endsomnia app helps users break unhealthy sleeping habits and create better ones.

Problem

More than a third of American adults are not getting enough sleep on a regular basis.

Research

According to a survey conducted by Deloitte, 81% of people use their phones within an hour before bedtime, even when the majority knows it can disturb the quality of their sleep.

After conducting a questionnaire study, the results reflected that 80.7% of the participants struggle with one or more problems with their sleep. 61.5% of the studied group are familiar with tips and are knowledgeable of how to improve their sleep, however only 11.6% of participants are practicing tips to improve their current sleep patterns.

insight

Insomnia can be triggered or perpetuated by a variety of behaviors and sleep patterns. Unhealthy lifestyles and sleep habits can be the originations of insomnia, and also make insomnia caused by other factors worse.

People are generally knowledgeable with good and bad sleeping habits, however, the missing component of improving sleep patterns and eliminating insomnia is motivation and practicing key tips.

Idea

If lifestyle and unhealthy sleep habits are the cause of insomnia, there are cognitive behavioral techniques and sleep hygiene tips that can help. Endsomnia aims to help users break unhealthy sleeping habits and create better ones for a good night's rest.

Screen Design

View Data

Users are able to interact with the "Analysis" page by scrolling through time and being able to see their sleep patterns. The circle conveniently changes colors based on the cycle of sleep they are in at any given time.

Set goals

On the "Habit" page, users will be asked to select their insomnia symptoms in order to receive tailored reminders dependant on their selections. After identifying insomnia problems, users will be able to select goals to work on to improve their sleep.

Learn & track habits

Clicking on each goal on the "Habit" page allows users to briefly see and learn about the advantages of performing each habit. Users are also able to track their progress.

Set bedtime / wake up time

When users input their desired wake up or bed time, Endomnia then provides suggested times to optimize for their REM and non-REM sleep cycles. Based on the users goal bedtime, Endsomnia can also calculate the best time to send reminders to users.

Receive reminders

Once users set their goals, Endsomnia will start to send out notifications and remind them to perform healthy habits and quit unhealthy ones. Endsomnia will send reminders to users and help them practice healthy sleeping habits at the best time possible. As an example, users can receive a notification 6 hours before bed to "Avoid Caffeine".

Practice habits

When users receive their reminders, they can slide up to see additional information and learn about the advantages of performing each habit. Users will swipe right when they accomplish a goal or swipe left to temporarily skip a reminder.

Get motivated

When users complete a goal, the application will send encouraging messages and motivate users to continue to achieve their goals and build healthy habits to one day Endsomnia.

Track History

On the "History" page, Users have access to all the sleep data and history which reflects the user's participation. The available information will help track and continuously achieve their set goals.
